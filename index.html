About Me

My aim is to help you become the best version of yourself and build your confidence in the gym.

From a young age I have always been involved in fitness as I grew up going to dance classes which pushed me to go to college to study dance. During this time I started to notice that my weight was deteriorating and even though my fitness levels were good, I lacked muscle and definition which lead to lack of confidence.

When I left college I began doing home workouts using a pair of dumbbells and a barbell to build up my strength.
I joined a local gym to develop my strength training, I began lifting heavier weights and building my self confidence, I started to track my calories to ensure I was eating enough to build muscle.

I decided that I wanted to return to college to study my Level 2 Fitness Instructing and Level 3 Personal Training, whilst I was doing this my passion for dance continued and I worked hard to qualify as a dance teacher.

Since then my love for the gym continues to grow and I am excited to see where my own journey takes me.
